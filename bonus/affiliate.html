Affiliate Link Generator

Follow the steps below to generate your custom sales page link.

Directions for Affiliates:

Sign Up for WarriorPlus:

  • Visit WarriorPlus and create an account.
  • Complete the sign-up process and verify the account through email.

Get Approved for the Specific Product:

  • Log in to WarriorPlus and navigate to the Affiliates section.
  • Search for your product by name in the WarriorPlus marketplace.
  • Submit a request for approval to promote the product.

Obtain the Affiliate Link:

  • Once approved, locate the unique affiliate link provided by WarriorPlus for your product.
  • This link is crucial for tracking sales and commissions.

Promote the Product:

  • Use the obtained affiliate link in all promotional materials, ensuring sales are tracked accurately.
