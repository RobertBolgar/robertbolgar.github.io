How to Sign Up and Get Approved on WarriorPlus

  1. Visit WarriorPlus and create an account.
  2. Log in to WarriorPlus and navigate to the "Affiliates" section, then "Offers" to find the product.
  3. Search for the product by name and click "Request Approval" to promote the product.
  4. Once approved, locate your unique affiliate link in the "Offers" section.
  5. Use this link in your promotions to ensure sales are tracked to your account.

For detailed guidance, refer to WarriorPlus support and documentation.
